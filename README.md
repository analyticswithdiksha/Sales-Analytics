# Sales-Analytics
Interactive Sales Analytics Dashboard built using Excel and Power BI for business performance analysis.
Project Overview

The Sales Analytics Dashboard is an interactive business intelligence project developed using Microsoft Excel and Power BI. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights, enabling stakeholders to monitor sales performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

The project focuses on analyzing sales across different regions, product categories, sales representatives, and sales channels while providing an intuitive and user-friendly dashboard experience.

Project Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance
Identify top-performing sales representatives
Compare sales across different regions
Track monthly revenue trends
Analyze product category performance
Compare online and retail sales channels
Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making
Tools Used
Microsoft Excel – Data Cleaning & Preparation
Microsoft Power BI – Data Visualization & Dashboard Creation

Key Business Insights
North region generated the highest sales revenue.
Clothing category contributed the largest share of total sales.
David emerged as the top-performing sales representative.
Online sales channel slightly outperformed retail sales.
Sales remained relatively stable throughout the year with noticeable peaks in January and October.
Business Recommendations
Focus marketing efforts on high-performing regions to maximize revenue.
Increase inventory for top-selling product categories.
Analyze the strategies of top-performing sales representatives and replicate best practices.
Strengthen online sales initiatives to drive further growth.
Monitor monthly sales trends to optimize promotional campaigns and inventory planning.
